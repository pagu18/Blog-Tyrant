30 Days of Web Development

By Pragya Dadhich
21 January 2020 | 5 min read


30 Days of Web Development provided an opportunity to kick start my journey in web development and get hands-on experience with Html, CSS, JS,Bootstrap and some basics of backend. This program provided the best content at no cost, including sessions from awesome speakers and also the skilled mentors who guided with their immense knowledge throughout the program.

It was organized by a total of 50+ DSCLeads over the world. Developer Student Club(DSC), are university based community groups for student interested in Google developer technlogies. Students from all undergraduate or graduate programs with an interest in growing as a developer are welcome. By joining DSC, students grow their knowledge in peer-to-peer learning environment and build solutions for local buisnesses and their community.

The first day started with Kick-off session that provided the whole agenda of 30 days which changed the normal lives to developer ones.The next session for Github boosted the track in such a way that for the first time I sticked to the full program. Furthuemore, the HTML session by Bhoomika Wavhal, CSS session by Joshua Poddoku, Bootstrap by Sanjana Shivakumar, JS by Praveen Kumar Purshothaman (#learn with fun) and the whole backend sessions by Anurag Sharan and Pranav Agarwal were remarkable. Alongwith the sessions , a beginner-friendly content was provided to acquire in-depth knowledge. For the First time ever, doing quizzez and projects was fun.
